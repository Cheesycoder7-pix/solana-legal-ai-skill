# Global Stablecoin Compliance Frameworks

This guide assists software engineers in analyzing core regulatory conditions for issuing digital payment tokens.

## 🇪🇺 European Union MiCA (Markets in Crypto-Assets) Compliance
- **Classification**: Stablecoins are split into Asset-Referenced Tokens (ARTs) and Electronic Money Tokens (EMTs).
- **Mandatory Logic**: EMT issuers must be authorized credit institutions or electronic money institutions.
- **Reserve Protocol**: Issuers must secure a 1:1 liquid reserve ratio held safely in independent custody accounts.

## 🇺🇸 US Regulatory Landscape
- **Framework Guidance**: Token issuance requires strict alignment with state-level money transmitter licenses (MTL) or NYDFS limited-purpose trust frameworks.
- **Auditing Standards**: Daily proof-of-reserves and independent monthly public accounting statements are mandatory to prevent enforcement flags.
