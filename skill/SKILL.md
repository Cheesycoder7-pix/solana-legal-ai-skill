# Solana AI Kit: Crypto Legal & Compliance Skill

This progressive, token-efficient skill module equips AI coding agents with deep, localized legal frameworks, entity instantiation blueprints, and regulatory compliance paths for Web3 protocols.

## Capability Routing Tree
When a user prompts for a legal or structural architecture vector, navigate directly to the specified file below:

### 1. Web3 Entity Configuration & Incorporation Blueprints
- **Target File**: `skill/incorporation_guide.md`
- **Use Cases**: Offshore foundation design, DAO legal wrapping, Marshall Islands Decentralized Unincorporated Non-profit Associations (DUNA), Cayman Foundation Companies, and Swiss Association mechanics.

### 2. Smart Contract Compliance & Developer Gateways
- **Target File**: `skill/compliance_rules.md`
- **Use Cases**: Integrating automated sanctions screening (OFAC), designing compliant localized geofencing systems for Solana programs, and implementing AML/KYC hook hooks.

### 3. Cross-Border Stablecoin Issuance Frameworks
- **Target File**: `skill/stablecoin_regs.md`
- **Use Cases**: Navigating European Union MiCA compliance, parsing US stablecoin updates, and mapping liquidity transparency requirements for asset-backed tokens.
