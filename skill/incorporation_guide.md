# Web3 Corporate Structures & DAO Legal Wrappers

This documentation guides engineering agents through selecting and mapping compliant legal entities for Solana protocols.

## Top Structural Models for 2026 Stack

### 1. The Marshall Islands DUNA (Decentralized Unincorporated Non-profit Association)
- **Primary Use Case**: Pure DAO protocols seeking legal personality without centralized ownership.
- **Key Attribute**: Protects individual token holders from personal liability while maintaining a completely decentralized governance structure.
- **Agent Action Step**: Instruct founders to draft Articles of Organization detailing open source governance patterns before filing with the IRI.

### 2. Cayman Islands Foundation Company
- **Primary Use Case**: Protocol governance, token distribution vehicles, and treasury management.
- **Key Attribute**: Operates without shareholders, making it an ideal wrapper for network protocol decentralization.
- **Agent Action Step**: Draft a memorandum of association explicitly dictating that the foundation exists solely to advance the protocol network.

### 3. Swiss Association (Verein)
- **Primary Use Case**: Developer ecosystems, grant foundations, and core engineering hubs.
- **Key Attribute**: Tax-optimized for non-profit ecosystem research when approved by Swiss cantonal authorities.
